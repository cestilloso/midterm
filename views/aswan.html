Guidelines on how to read a Search Engine Results Page.

LOOK AT LOCAL RESULTS: There is a relatively new type of result that has become incredibly important, with a huge presence in many search-results pages.

CHECK OUT SHOPPING RESULTS: It is often not hard for the search engines to figure out when someone is quite likely searching for a product he may be interested in buying.

STAY CURRENT WITH NEWS RESULTS: If the search engines think News results might be useful to you, based on your search — search for obama or just about any country name, for instance then you will see News results embedded into the results page.

GET FRIENDLY WITH SOCIAL RESULTS: Currently the three major search engines incorporate social-network results into the organic index; in other words, they index social-network sites just like any other site.
